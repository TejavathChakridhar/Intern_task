Cause Lists

Cause list displayed may differ from the actual cause list. For further queries, contact court administrator.

How to 1. Select State, District and Court Complex of the Cause list to be displayed. 2. Select the entry from the Court Name Select box, which shows the court number, the judge name and the designation of the judge for displaying the Cause list. 3. Select the Cause list Date from the calendar control. 4. Enter the Captcha (the 5 digit numbers shown on the screen) in the text box provided. 5. Click on the Civil or Criminal button, to accordingly display the Civil or Criminal Cause list of the selected Court and Date.
